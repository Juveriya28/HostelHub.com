OUR LOCATIONS

Whether you're looking for a peaceful retreat or an exciting urban adventure, our hostels are located in the heart of some of the most vibrant and dynamic cities in the world. These locations are carefully selected to give you easy access to the best local attractions and cultural experiences.

Affordable hostels in India offer a unique cultural experience and the chance to meet other travelers. Many are located in historic buildings, have colorful decor, and provide private rooms and dorms. Free breakfast and Wi-Fi are often included. Popular options include Shine Executive and Pearl Lotus Hostel.

Hostels are a popular accommodation option for budget travelers in South America. Many are located in historic buildings, have communal areas such as courtyards or rooftop terraces, and offer private rooms and dorms. Free breakfast and Wi-Fi are often included. Popular options include los Potis and Loki Hostel.

Thailand's affordable hostels come in various sizes and offer a mix of private rooms and dorms. Many have communal areas like outdoor terraces, kitchens, and lounges. Free breakfast and Wi-Fi are often included, along with organized tours and activities. Popular options include Lub d Hostel and Thai Cat Hostel.
